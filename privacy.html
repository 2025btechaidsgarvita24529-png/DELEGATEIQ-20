Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect your name, email, and school during registration. We also collect usage data like research queries and conference history you add to your profile.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to provide personalized MUN assistance, display your profile and badges, and remember your committee context. We do not sell your data to third parties.

3. Data Storage

Account data is stored in Google Firebase. Profile preferences are stored in your browser's localStorage. AI queries are processed by Google Gemini and are not stored by DelegateIQ.

4. Third Party Services

We use Firebase (Google) for auth and storage, Google Gemini for AI responses, and Wikipedia API for research. Each has its own privacy policy.

5. Contact

For privacy concerns: delegateiq2026@gmail.com
